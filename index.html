As the CEO of this venture, I specialized in developing immersive VR and AR experiences tailored to various industries, such as construction, energy, and retail. Through this endeavor, I gained invaluable expertise in product development, leadership, communication, and project management.



Driven by my desire for growth and new experiences, I made the decision to relocate to Silicon Valley. The program allowed me to engage with talented engineers from diverse backgrounds, fostering a global perspective and accelerating my professional growth.



Inspired by my time at 42 Silicon Valley in 2019, I ventured into the realm of freelance coding. This decision not only provided me with opportunities to collaborate with a wide range of businesses but also enabled me to cultivate a versatile skill set.
